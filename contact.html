Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you repair my car?

Most repairs are completed within 24-48 hours. However, complex issues may take longer. We'll provide you with an estimated timeline after diagnosing your vehicle.

Do you offer warranties on your repairs?

Yes! We offer a 12-month warranty on all parts and labor. This ensures you have peace of mind knowing your repair is protected.

Is the pickup and delivery service really free?

Absolutely! Our pickup and delivery service is available within Ikeja and surrounding areas. There are no hidden charges for this convenience.

What types of vehicles do you service?

We service all makes and models of vehicles, including Japanese, European, and American cars. We also work on hybrids and electric vehicles.

How do I pay for the services?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and POS payments. Payment is due when your vehicle is delivered back to you after repairs are completed.

Need Emergency Repair?

We offer 24/7 emergency services for urgent car issues. Don't hesitate to call us anytime!
